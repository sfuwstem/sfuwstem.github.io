Hi, we are the Women in STEM club at SFU.

We are allied with students of all different backgrounds and identities. Our goal is to create a supportive environment in all fields of STEM. Our main focus is to connect with women at SFU through various outreach and networking events.

We work together to plan for events where students at SFU can expand their skills and knowledge. We aspire to create a positive impact for students in their careers as they explore...

SCIENCE | TECHNOLOGY | ENGINEERING | MATHEMATICS

What does SFUWSTEM do?



SOCIAL MIXERS

We want the SFU community to feel connected. We hold mixers so that students can find their peers in similar studies and interests. Hosting these events allow a place for people to gather to in a more casual setting outside of lecture halls.

PANELS

Our goal is to provide useful information that can help advance the careers of students. Panels feature guests from various backgrounds in STEM that want to share their experience in their respective fields.

OUTREACH

We work with other SFU clubs, societies, associations, unions and community organizations to plan events and share opportunities for our members to keep them informed about networking and outreach events that interest them.



To stay up to date with our events, follow us on Instagram, Facebook join our Discord. You can also get in contact with us by sending an email at sfuwstem@sfu.ca.
